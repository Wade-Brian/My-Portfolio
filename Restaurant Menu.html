Wade Delicacies Menu

Appetizers

  • Samosa

    Crispy pastry filled with spiced beef or vegetable , served with tangy tamarind sauce.

  • Bhajia

    Potato slices coated in spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried

  • Mahamri ya Nazi

    Swahili coconut doughnuts, best paired with chai.

Main Courses

  • Nyama Choma

    Grilled goat or beef served with kachumbari and ugali. (Kenya's national dish)

  • Pilau

    Fragrant spiced rice cooked with beef or chicken.

  • Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

    Maize meal served with sauteed collard greens.

Desserts

  • Mandazi

    Soft, slightly sweet fried dough often enjoyed with tea.

  • Kaimati

    Sweet dumplings coated with sugar syrup.

  • Mandazi ya Mkate wa Sinia

    Pillowy Swahili doughnuts dusted with cinnamon sugar.

  • Matoke and Ice cream

    Carmelized plantains wirh vanilla ice cream.

  • Mango Delight

    Fresh Mango slices served with a drizzle of honey and lime.

Drinks & Beverages

  • Chai

    Kenyan- styled spiced tea brewed with milk and sugar.

  • Mursik

    Traditional fermented milk popular amon the Kalenjin community.

  • Dawa

    Kenya's famous cocktail: vodka, honey, lime and crushed ice.

  • Madafu

    Fresh coconut water, served straight from the shell.

  • Tusker lager

    Kenya's iconic beer, brewed since 1922.

  • Soda

    Popular soft drinks including Coca-Cola, Fanta and Sprite.
